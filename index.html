Architectural Glass

Energy-efficient windows and facades that enhance building performance. We design glass products that are lighter yet strong enough to be used in various structural applications, from skyscrapers to pedestrian bridges.

Interior Glass

Stylish and sustainable options for partitions, doors, and decor. Also glass with antimicrobial coatings for use in healthcare settings, public transportation, and high-touch surfaces to reduce the spread of germs and bacteria.

Automotive Glass

Durable and eco-friendly glass solutions for vehicles. We develop advanced automotive glass that offers features like heads-up displays (HUDs), augmented reality, or enhanced UV protection.

Specialty Glass
