DAVID ODUNAYO OYEWOGA

Chief Executive Officer

David is a seasoned Industrial Chemist with over 30 years of extensive experience in executive leadership and strategic management. He is a visionary leader known for his ability to drive organizational growth and optimize operational efficiency. He is currently the CEO of Ecoglass Nigeria Limited. His career spans four decades in the chemical and glass manufacturing industries, having worked in various capacities at Toyo Glass (now Beta Glass) from 1975 to 1987, Oluwa Glass PLC from 1987 to 2000, West African Glass Industry from 2000 to 2004, Prime Chemical from 2005 to 2010, and Hissignatures Global Investment Ltd from 2005 to date.

As the CEO of Hissignatures Global Investment Ltd since 2013, David has developed and executed comprehensive business strategies, managed high-level stakeholder relationships, and led executive teams to achieve corporate objectives. His role involves overseeing all operations and business activities to ensure they produce the desired results and align with the overall strategy and mission of the company. He also ensures adherence to industry standards, legal guidelines, and in-house policies to maintain the company’s legality and business ethics. Over the years, he has garnered experience in all departments, including Technical, Production, and Marketing, rising through the ranks to the level of executive director.

David’s notable achievements include contributing to and participating in drawing the maiden glass specifications by the Nigeria Standards Organisation in 1977. He pioneered the conversion and sourcing of local substitutes for imported raw materials, such as replacing Soda Ash with Magadi Soda and Trona, Dolomite with beneficiated dolomite, Limestone with local limestone, Alumina with feldspar, Coke powder with carbon from cellulose, and Osmose Clay with locally formulated clay and other materials.

Previously, as a Quality Control Manager at Oluwa Glass Company between 1987-2003, David oversaw all quality control operations, ensuring safe and efficient production while meeting environmental and regulatory standards. He developed and implemented quality control systems, inspected and tested materials, equipment, processes, and products to ensure quality standards were met. David was instrumental in preparing and managing quality control budgets, identifying cost-saving opportunities, and controlling operational expenditures while maintaining high productivity and quality standards.

He is highly knowledgeable about implementing quality control measures, optimizing production workflows, coordinating large-scale equipment maintenance, and managing diverse teams. David is an exceptional leader who fosters a positive organizational culture, while engaging and making a positive impact within local communities. Additionally, he serves as the Head of the Ibelefu Community in Ogun State, Nigeria.
